About Us

The digital divide is a common term that relates income and the ability to stay up to date with modern technologies. In this day and age, all people should have the opportunity to have access to reliable computers. Snooze Cub Computers was made to level the playing field for those who can not afford to keep up with rising costs. Education should not be set aside due to income restrictions. The computers we provide are sourced from our generous local businesses and community members, so that all people have the chance to stay up to date, keep in touch, and enjoy the digital age, without the burden of high costs.
